Business to be renamed Rockmart Fuel Containment MIAMI, FL and ROCKMART, GA / January 27, 2026 / HEICO Corporation (NYSE: HEI.A and HEI) today announced that its Electronic Technologies Group completed its previously announced acquisition of Axillon Aerospace’s Fuel Containment Business.  Axillon Fuel Containment will be renamed Rockmart Fuel Containment, LLC (“Rockmart”).
